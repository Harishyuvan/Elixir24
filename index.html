Welcome to our College Symposium 2024!

We are thrilled to invite you to our College Symposium 2024, A dynamic platform designed to ignite intellectual curiosity, foster innovation, and shape the future leaders of tomorrow. Organized by Karpaga Vinayaga College of Engineering And Technology, This symposium promises a stimulating blend of academic discourse, professional networking, and creative exploration.


Event Overview:

  • Date:April 10th 2024
  • Time: 9.00 A.M-3.30 P.M
  • Location: KVCET Engineering College
  • Entry Fee:150 Rs
  • Registration : Registration Form Link


About the event:

This year's symposium revolves around the theme "Igniting Minds, Shaping Futures," emphasizing the power of education, innovation, and collaboration in driving positive change in our rapidly evolving world. Through a series of keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and interactive sessions, participants will delve into a myriad of topics ranging from technology and sustainability to entrepreneurship and social justice.
